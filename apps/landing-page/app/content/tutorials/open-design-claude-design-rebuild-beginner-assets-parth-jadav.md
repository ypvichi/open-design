## What is Open Design?

Open Design is an open-source, local-first, agent-native design platform — a free alternative to Claude Design. Its key difference is the engine: instead of keeping you inside one vendor's paid limits, it lets you **bring your own AI** — Gemini or ChatGPT — so the same automation costs you nothing extra beyond a subscription you already have.

- **Open source, Apache-2.0**, runs on your machine.
- **Bring your own engine** via a local CLI bridge (Codex CLI), so you are not stacking another monthly bill.
- **One design system, everywhere** — the same brand rules attach to decks, docs, social, video, and prototypes.

## The problem: AI assets that don't match your brand

AI tools like Claude Design are genuinely powerful — they can turn a YouTube transcript into a 23-slide deck in seconds. But they default to that generic dark theme, disconnected from your colors, your fonts, your vibe. Fixing each asset by hand, every time, is exactly the friction that kills the workflow.

## The fix: a hub-and-spoke workflow

Instead of re-prompting the AI to "remember" your brand on every asset, you build **one central hub** — a design system — and spin every asset out from it as a spoke. Build it once, apply it everywhere. That single idea is what makes the whole thing scale.

## Step 1 — Build the design system (your brand DNA)

Think of the design system as your brand's DNA: colors, fonts, spacing, even the exact roundness of your buttons, written down in one place. Setup is deliberately easy — no overpriced agency brand kit required:

1. Give the AI your **brand name** and a **one-sentence mission** (e.g. "a community of curious AI folks called Staying Ahead").
2. **Upload a screenshot** of your current colors and fonts.
3. In about five minutes you have a ready-to-use rulebook — and you never type a hex code by hand again.

## Step 2 — Convert the deck with one command

Now the magic trick. Take that generic 23-slide deck and type a single sentence: **"convert the whole deck to our design system."** The moody default vanishes and every slide instantly adopts your exact colors and fonts — zero manual editing. Because the brand rules are now hardwired into the agent, you can scale from here.

## The five spokes, from one source

With the design system attached like an invisible layer, one source spins out into five on-brand assets:

- **Slide deck** — the branded 23-slide version you just converted.
- **Infographic** — switch the format to a document and ask for a one-page roadmap; it generates a branded infographic with no layout or color input needed.
- **Social carousel** — use the repurpose option to turn the one-pager into an Instagram carousel, slide by slide, ready to post.
- **Animated launch video** — ask for a kinetic text animation; the native engine outputs clean HTML/CSS motion that inherits your typography and colors. (For complex layer-by-layer generation you can bridge to connectors like Higgsfield, but the built-in engine handles clean promo video on its own.)
- **Landing page prototype** — a polished, interactive page with your logo, a headline, a working join form, a brand-colored button, and a social-proof badge.

When it is time to ship the page for real, exporting your design system first means the agent stops burning tokens redesigning from scratch — it grabs your pre-approved building blocks and pushes the design into a website builder or into your coding agent.

## Do the whole thing for free in Open Design

Everything above assumes you are paying for Claude. Open Design runs the **identical** hub-and-spoke automation for free. Two things make that work:

- **Bring your own engine.** Route an existing ChatGPT or Gemini subscription into Open Design through the **Codex CLI** bridge. The extra cost to run it is exactly zero.
- **Clone your brand DNA — don't rebuild it.** You do not start over. In Claude Design, export your design system as a **ZIP**; in Open Design, import that ZIP. Your colors, fonts, and whole vibe come across, and you are generating new spokes immediately.

## FAQ

**What is the hub-and-spoke workflow?** You build one design system (the hub) and generate every asset — deck, infographic, carousel, video, landing page — from it as a spoke, so brand consistency is automatic instead of manual.

**How do I set up a design system?** Give the AI your brand name and a one-line mission, upload a screenshot of your colors and fonts, and it produces a reusable rulebook in about five minutes.

**Is Open Design free?** Yes — it is open source and runs on your own engine. Connect an existing ChatGPT or Gemini subscription via the Codex CLI and there is no extra per-token bill.

**Can I move my brand from Claude Design?** Yes. Export your design system as a ZIP from Claude Design and import it into Open Design; your colors, fonts, and components carry over.

**Can it make video, not just static assets?** Yes. Ask for an animated launch video and the engine produces HTML/CSS kinetic text that inherits your brand type and colors.
