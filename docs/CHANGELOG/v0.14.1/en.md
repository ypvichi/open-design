## Silent updates

You can now allow Open Design to apply a downloaded update silently the next
time the app starts. Enable the option while installing an update or manage it
later in Settings.

The preference remains off until you explicitly confirm an installation. It
only affects the automatic update check performed at startup; periodic checks
and manual installer actions keep their existing behavior.
