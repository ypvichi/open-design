Three costs compounding today

Seat fees, lock-in,
and lost leverage

Per-seat SaaS tax

$500-$1,200 per designer per year across Figma, Claude Design, and adjacent AI add-ons — a bill that scales with headcount, not with output.

Closed-model lock-in

Claude Design and similar tools hard-code one model vendor. You can't swap providers when pricing or quality shifts, and you can't self-host.

Assets never leave the vendor's cloud

Every prototype, deck, and brand asset lives on someone else's servers — procurement inherits a permanent data-residency and security review.

Across a 40-person design org, that's roughly $38K-$48K a year in seat fees alone — before counting the AI tooling most teams already pay for separately.
