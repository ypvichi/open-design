BRAND AUDIT
The Diagnosis

BRAND
IN
PIECES.

Across 42 live pages, the Cairn wordmark appeared in 11 different croppings, three of them stretched off their original aspect ratio.

Product photography sat on four different creams, and the trail-marker orange shifted hue between the app icon, hangtags, and paid ads.

This system replaces guesswork with four locked rules: logo, color, type, and grid, enforced page by page.
