The Headline Answer

Runway holds at 18 months without a new raise

The Answer

Structurally Low Burn Is the Reason Runway Holds

  • BYOK shifts an estimated $2.1M/yr of model-inference cost onto users, off our P&L entirely
  • Apache-2.0 core lets 300+ community contributors ship features we never pay engineering time for
  • Local-first architecture removes the cloud-hosting bill that would otherwise scale with every new user
  • Net effect: burn multiple sits at 0.4x the industry median for our ARR stage

Before · Traditional Cost Model

Cloud Inference On Our Books

  • Model inference fully on the company's P&L: ~$2.1M/yr
  • Compute scales linearly with every new user
  • Single vendor, no BYOK, no cost pass-through
  • Effective burn: ~$310K/month

After · Open Design's Structure

Cost Shifted Off the P&L

  • BYOK: users bring their own model keys, inference cost off our books
  • Local-first: no per-seat hosting bill to carry
  • Apache-2.0 core: community ships ~40% of new features unpaid
  • Effective burn: ~$61K/month (-80%)
