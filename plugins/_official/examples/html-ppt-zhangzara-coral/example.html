01 / Market Moment
DEVELOPER TRUST IS BUILT IN PUBLIC, NOT IN A DEMO
Claude Design proved in April 2026 that an LLM can produce real design artifacts — but locked behind a closed, paid, cloud-only product. Open Design ships that same capability as Apache-2.0, local-first, BYOK software. The fastest path to category ownership isn't paid acquisition — it's a visible, repeatable growth loop across GitHub, Discord, and X that developers can watch, join, and re-share.
